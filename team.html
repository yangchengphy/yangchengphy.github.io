Yang Cheng

Assistant Professor, Electrical and Computer Engineering, UMass Lowell

Yang Cheng is a spintronics and quantum-device researcher. His research spans antiferromagnetic dynamics, voltage-controlled magnetic memory, neuromorphic computing hardware, cryogenic spintronic devices, and van der Waals quantum materials.
